Best Brownies

Description

This brownie recipe makes rich, fudgy brownies that always turn out well. They're topped with homemade chocolate frosting to make the best brownies ever!

Ingredients

  • Brownies
    • Butter
    • White sugar
    • Eggs
    • Vanilla Extract
    • Cocoa powder
    • All-purpose flour
    • Salt
    • Baking powder
  • Frosting
    • Butter
    • Cocoa powder
    • Honey
    • Vanilla extract
    • Confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Make the brownies
    1. Melt the butter on the stove
    2. Remove the heat
    3. Add the remaining wet ingredients
    4. Beat in the dry ingredients
    5. Spread the batter in the pan and bake
  2. Frost the brownies
    1. Stir the frosting ingredients together until well-combined
    2. Frost the brownies while they are still warm
    3. Cut into squares when cool
