World's Best Lasagna

Description

An incredible customizable recipe where you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the recipe, you can do that as-well.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
    5. How to layer the the lasagna
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat slices
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top the remaining Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
